The origins of the Pizzeria

Uncle Tanner's was founded by a man named Alexander Mertsch on October 6th, 2027.

Ever since he was a young lad, Mertsch was always a big guy for pizza - it was his absolute favorite food that he simply could not get enough of.

This personality trait of his would persist all the way up to adulthood and it would be the pillar of inspiration for him to open his business.

Mertsch would name his business after his younger brother Tanner since he felt it would give the establishment a more personal attachment to him and his family.

Our Mission

Our identity as a brand is centered around one specific phrase: Go big, or go home!

Uncle Tanner's aim to provide pizzas you would never see on any other menu while still offering you with top quality versions of the kind you are used to.

Our pizzas can range from size variations to full on Pinapple on Pizza, yes, we are serious.

We believe that consumer choice makes businesses more appealing and opens people up to trying out new things they may have not of thought of before.

We encourage you to look through our menu and see what crazy combinations you can come up with!
